Why IDX Grip

Built for investors who do their own research.

Real investment insight doesn't come from chasing headlines. It comes from understanding what drives prices over time — the earnings surprises, the structural shifts, the sector rotations that most retail platforms miss entirely.

IDX Grip connects everything: 5-year price timelines with annotated corporate events, a live-calculated market heatmap across every IDX sector, and a multi-factor stock screener that ranks tickers by Value, Momentum, and Quality.

Every tool is built to surface the kind of data that institutional investors rely on, packaged for the individual investor.
